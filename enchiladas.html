Enchiladas

Description

Do you love chicken enchiladas as much as we do? Here's one of our favorite chicken enchiladas recipes!

These enchiladas can work for an easy mid-week meal and can even be made ahead.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tomato
  • Organic Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Tortillas
  • Jalapenos
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Place tortillas in tray
  3. Layer ground beef, cheese, tomato, Jalapenos, and cilantro
  4. Place in oven and leave for 45 minutes
  5. Take out of oven, let sit for 8 minutes and enjoy!
